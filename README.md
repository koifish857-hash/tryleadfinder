# LeadFinder MVP

A lightweight static MVP for validating a paid B2B lead finder aimed at solo consultants, freelancers, and micro agencies.

## What This Tests

- Whether micro agencies want a weekly list of scored local business leads.
- Whether problem-based lead signals feel more valuable than generic directories.
- Whether the initial pricing range of `$39-$199/mo` is plausible.

## Run Locally

Open `index.html` in a browser.

## First Real Launch Steps

1. Replace the `mailto:` links with a real payment or booking link.
2. Connect the email capture form to Tally, Typeform, ConvertKit, Beehiiv, or a simple backend.
3. Manually fulfill the first 5-10 paid lead packs before building scraping automation.
4. Start with one niche, such as dentists in Texas or Shopify stores missing retention basics.
